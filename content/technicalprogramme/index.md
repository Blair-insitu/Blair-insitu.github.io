        AUV2026 will be a single track symposium featuring over 40 oral presentations and 40 poster presentations alongside and industry exhibition. 

        There will be keynote talks, a panel session, facility tours and a gala dinner. 

        Details of the programme will be posted here. 

        All authors should have been contacted by our technical committee on their presentation format. If you submitted to our Journal or Standard track, and you are unsure whether/how you will be presenting, get in touch [auv2026@soton.ac.uk](mailto:auv2026@soton.ac.uk)

        Posters should be prepared to fit a A0 landscape poster board and printed posters should be brought to the venue.

        Presentations should be 12 minutes, with an additional 3minutes for questions. There will be a windows PC with the latest version of microsoft powerpoint and adobe PDF installed - you need to make sure your presentation is uploaded before the start of your session. There will also be an HDMI input if you prefer to use your own laptop.
